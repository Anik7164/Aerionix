Database Overview

This project includes the following tables:

  • Aircraft: Details about aircraft including model, capacity, and more.
  • Airports:Lists airports with their code, name, city, and country.
  • Baggage: Baggage details associated with each flight and passenger.
  • Bookings: Booking statuses and details for each passenger.
  • Catering:Stores catering information for flights, including menu and date.
  • Checkin:Captures passenger check-in records with time and flight information.
  • Crew: Information about crew members, their roles, and associated flights.
  • Flight Crew:Tracks crew assignments to specific flights with their roles.
  • Flights: Flight schedules, including departure and arrival times.
  • Flight Schedules:Maintains flight schedules with departure and arrival times.
  • Flight_Stats:Records flight statistics including delays and duration.
  • Fuel Details:Tracks fuel records for each aircraft, including date and quantity.
  • Flight Maintenance:Logs aircraft maintenance records with dates and descriptions.
  • Passengers: Passenger information and their bookings.
  • Routes:Defines routes between two airports and the distance between them.
